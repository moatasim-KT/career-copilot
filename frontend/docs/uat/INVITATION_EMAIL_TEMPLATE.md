## What is Career Copilot?

Career Copilot is a comprehensive job search and application tracking platform designed to help job seekers organize their job search, track applications, and gain insights into their job search progress.

## What is User Acceptance Testing?

User Acceptance Testing is the final phase of testing where real users like you test the application to ensure it meets their needs and expectations. Your feedback will be invaluable in helping us improve the product before launch.
